What is a

Brain-computer

interface?

Imagine you could play a video game just by thinking about your moves. Or type on a computer without using your hands. Well, you could do all of those, and more with a brain-computer interface (BCI)! They work by reading signals from our brain, and translating them to commands a computer can understand. But it isn’t just a party trick, It could change human society forever.
